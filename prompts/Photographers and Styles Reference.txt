Quick Stable Diffusion Prompt Patterns
======================================

Classic street (Winogrand/Gilden): "35mm BW street, Leica M4 28mm, Tri-X 400 pushed, harsh midday sun, on-camera flash, f/16 zone focus, deep DOF, candid motion blur."

Quiet color (Eggleston/Herzog): "35mm color, Kodachrome look, muted Southern light, slight grain, 50mm, f/8, suburban vernacular, dye-transfer tonality."

Telephoto color street (Leiter): "Kodachrome color through rain-soaked glass, 135mm, shallow DOF f/2.8, reflections and bold reds, compressed perspective."

Large-format portrait (Avedon/Penn): "8x10 studio portrait, 360mm, white seamless, crisp strobe, f/16, microcontrast, minimal pose, medium grain."

Cinematic tableaux (Crewdson/Wall): "8x10 staged suburban night scene, complex film lighting, 300mm perspective, f/8, atmospheric haze, hyperreal color."

New Topographics (Becher/Baltz): "4x5 BW industrial façade, overcast sky, orthographic framing, 150mm, f/32, neutral tonality."

High-contrast BW Japan (Moriyama): "gritty BW, 28mm compact, high ISO grain, blur and glare, f/2.8, aggressive contrast, urban night."

Soft pastel poetic (Kawauchi): "6x6 pastel color, 80mm, f/2.8 shallow DOF, gentle light, everyday lyricism."

Hard sun fashion (Ritts/Newton): "medium-format fashion, 150mm, hard noon sun, deep shadows, f/11, sculptural pose, crisp edges."

Monumental color landscape (Gursky/Burtynsky): "elevated industrial vista, long lens compression, meticulous detail, f/11, large-format clarity, cool palette."

Notes for Prompting
===================
- If "film" is not supported, emulate: "Tri-X 400 look, pushed one stop," or "Kodachrome 64 color palette."
- For LF depth and sharpness, set "tiny aperture f/32–f/64, tripod, long exposure."
- For 1980s flash street, specify "on-camera flash, 1/125s, f/16, close focus 1–2 m."
- For telephoto compression, add "85–200mm, background compression."

Camera/Lens Information for Camera Angle Section
=================================================
Street/Documentary: 28mm, 35mm, 50mm, 85mm
Portrait/Fashion: 85mm, 105mm, 135mm, 150mm, 360mm
Landscape/Architecture: 90mm, 150mm, 300mm, 360mm
Large Format: 210mm, 300mm, 360mm

Focus Information for Focus Section
====================================
Street/Documentary: zone focus, deep DOF f/8–f/16, selective focus
Portrait/Fashion: shallow DOF f/1.4–f/5.6, sharp focus on subject
Landscape/Architecture: deep DOF f/22–f/64, everything in focus, hyperfocal distance
Large Format: everything in focus, f/32–f/64, tripod required

Lighting Information for Lighting Section
==========================================
Street/Documentary: available light, natural light, on-camera flash, harsh midday sun
Portrait/Fashion: studio strobes, white seamless, hard sun, soft natural light
Landscape/Architecture: golden hour, blue hour, overcast sky, long exposures
Contemporary: available light, fluorescent, neon, ambient lighting, complex film lighting
