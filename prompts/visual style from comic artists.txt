Here are notable American comic book artists organized by era:
1960s-1970s:

Jack Kirby - Defined Marvel's visual style, co-created numerous characters including X-Men and Fantastic Four
Steve Ditko - Co-created Spider-Man and Doctor Strange, known for surreal, psychedelic art
Neal Adams - Revolutionized Batman's look, brought realistic anatomy and dynamic poses
John Romita Sr. - Defined Spider-Man's classic look after Ditko, became Marvel's art director

1980s-1990s:

John Byrne - Famous X-Men and Fantastic Four runs, known for clean, detailed style
Frank Miller - Revolutionized Daredevil and Batman, introduced noir and manga influences
Todd McFarlane - Redefined Spider-Man's look, created Spawn, known for detailed webs and capes
Jim Lee - X-Men artist turned industry leader, known for dynamic action and detailed musculature
Rob Liefeld - Created Deadpool, defined extreme 90s style with exaggerated proportions

2000s-Present:

Alex Ross - Photorealistic painted style, famous for Kingdom Come and Marvels
Sara Pichelli - Created Miles Morales Spider-Man, known for expressive characters
David Aja - Minimalist style on Hawkeye, innovative page layouts
Greg Capullo - Batman artist, detailed horror-influenced style
Fiona Staples - Saga artist, distinctive digital painting technique

DC Comics:

George Pérez - Legendary Crisis on Infinite Earths artist, detailed crowd scenes
Ivan Reis - Known for Aquaman and Green Lantern, photorealistic style
Francis Manapul - Flash artist, innovative watercolor techniques
Jim Aparo - Definitive Batman artist of the 1970s-80s
Curt Swan - Superman's classic look from Silver Age through Bronze Age
Amanda Conner - Known for Power Girl and Harley Quinn, expressive style

Marvel Comics:

John Buscema - Conan and Silver Surfer artist, classic Marvel house style
Stuart Immonen - Known for Ultimate Spider-Man, adaptable style
Chris Bachalo - Distinctive style on X-Men and Doctor Strange
Mark Bagley - Record run on Ultimate Spider-Man
David Finch - Dark, detailed style on Moon Knight and New Avengers
Jerome Opeña - Uncanny X-Force artist, cinematic storytelling
Steve McNiven - Civil War artist, detailed realistic style
Olivier Coipel - Thor and House of M artist, dynamic compositions
Leinil Yu - Known for Secret Invasion, unique angular style
Mike Deodato Jr. - Dark Avengers artist, photorealistic approach
Independent/Both Publishers:

Bill Sienkiewicz - Revolutionary mixed media style, New Mutants and Moon Knight
Sean Murphy - Known for Batman: White Knight, detailed mechanical drawings
Becky Cloonan - First female Batman artist, indie comics pioneer
Ryan Ottley - Invincible artist, dynamic action sequences
Daniel Warren Johnson - Wonder Woman: Dead Earth, intense detail and energy
Jorge Jiménez - Current Batman artist, manga-influenced style
Russell Dauterman - Thor and X-Men artist, fashion-forward designs
Kenneth Rocafort - Unique architectural elements, tech designs
Andrea Sorrentino - Old Man Logan, innovative panel layouts
Stephanie Hans - Die artist, painted digital style

Rising Stars:

Peach Momoko - Japanese-influenced cover artist
Dan Mora - Detective Comics, Once & Future artist
Joe Bennett - Immortal Hulk run
Mirka Andolfo - Distinctive character designs
Marco Checchetto - Daredevil artist, cinematic style
Comic Legends:

Bernie Wrightson - Horror master, Swamp Thing co-creator
Michael Turner - Founded Aspen Comics, known for fluid style
Tim Sale - Batman: Long Halloween, distinctive noir style
Dave McKean - Mixed media covers for Sandman
Travis Charest - Detailed linework, famous WildC.A.T.s run

Current Masters:

Clay Mann - Batman/Catwoman, realistic figure work
David Marquez - Batman/Superman, digital precision
Nicola Scott - Wonder Woman and Black Magick artist
Dustin Nguyen - Batman: Li'l Gotham, watercolor style
Lee Bermejo - Batman: Damned, painted realistic style
Gary Frank - Doomsday Clock, expressive characters
Mitch Gerads - Mister Miracle, innovative coloring
Jen Bartel - Cover artist, neon-noir aesthetic
Veterans:

Jim Starlin - Created Thanos, cosmic art style
Walt Simonson - Legendary Thor run, dynamic layouts
Brian Bolland - Famous for Batman: The Killing Joke covers
Dave Gibbons - Watchmen artist, technical precision

Current Generation:

Esad Ribić - Thor: God of Thunder, painted style
Joshua Middleton - Cover artist, painted portraits
Chris Samnee - Daredevil, classic storytelling
Jock - Batman covers, stark minimalism
Rafael Albuquerque - American Vampire creator
Otto Schmidt - Green Arrow, dynamic action scenes
